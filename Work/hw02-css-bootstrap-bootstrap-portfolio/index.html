About Me

Hi, I'm Nick! I'm a husband and father of two boys in Woodbury, MN. Before that I was a freelance web designer, and I'm currently studying at U of M CCAPS in Saint Paul to expand my skills in the direction of full stack web development. My interests are design, art, games, obsessive sorting and cataloguing, and spelunking the dark corners of popular and unpopular culture. I am left-handed.
